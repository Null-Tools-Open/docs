## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is Null Board free?">
    **Yes!** Null Board is completely free for local use. You can create unlimited local boards and save them to your device.

    We offer paid plans (Monthly, Annual, One-Time) that unlock **Cloud Sync**, **Real-time Collaboration**, and advanced AI features.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use Null Board offline?">
    Yes! You can save your boards as `.json` files locally and load them anytime, even without an internet connection. We are also working on native desktop apps for a fully offline experience.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is my data safe?">
    If you use local saving, your data never leaves your device. For cloud workspaces, we use industry-standard encryption to keep your data secure.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I report a bug?">
    You can report bugs by opening an issue on our [GitHub repository](https://github.com/Null-Tools-Open/nullboard).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
