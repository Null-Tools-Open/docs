## Overview

**NullBoard** is designed to be a flexible, infinite workspace where you can organize thoughts, plan projects, and collaborate with your team without boundaries. Whether you're brainstorming, diagramming, or just taking notes, NullBoard adapts to your workflow.

## Roadmap

We are actively working on the following features for future releases:
- [ ] **Mobile Support**: Optimized touch interactions and responsive layouts for tablets and phones.
- [ ] **Advanced Shapes**: More flowchart shapes, connectors, and mind-mapping tools.
- [ ] **Plugin System**: An API to allow community-created tools and extensions.
- [ ] **Desktop Apps**: Native Electron-based applications for offline use.
